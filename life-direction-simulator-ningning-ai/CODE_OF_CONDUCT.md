# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful, thoughtful, and supportive place for people who are navigating life choices.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving feedback with clarity and kindness
- Sharing personal experience without turning it into universal truth
- Respecting different life situations, constraints, and values

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Personal attacks or harassment
- Shame-based advice
- Discriminatory language
- Encouraging reckless, high-risk decisions without context

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate these standards.
